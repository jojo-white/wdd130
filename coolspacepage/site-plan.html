Our Solar System

Our solar system has 8 main planets (although I think Pluto should count as a main planet too, so I'll include it on my website :P), ranging in all different sizes and colors. From closest to the sun to furthest from the sun, the planets are: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, and Pluto. You will be able to learn all sorts of information about them, from their size, mass, color, length of day and year, type of planet, and so much more!

Voyager I and Voyager II

Voyager I and Voyager II are space probes we have sent on the way out of our solar system, studying the planets of our solar system as it makes it's way out of the system. We included information of our whereabouts in space in case alien life were to find it, as well as what we are and what we do (on a gold plate, so that's cool). The official NASA website has data showing a live count of how far away they are from earth, but I'll include it here too so that you can see it for yourself.
